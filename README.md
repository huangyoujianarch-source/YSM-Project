# 📊 Participation, Engagement & Outcome Analysis

## 🔍 Project Overview

This project explores how participant engagement relates to outcome scores across different demographic groups and time periods.

It focuses on answering:

- Who participates in the program?
- Does higher engagement lead to better outcomes?
- Does participation drive improvement over time?

---

## 🎯 Key Insight

> Participants without engagement tend to start with higher scores, but highly engaged participants demonstrate the strongest improvement over time.

### 📌 KPI

**+0.48 average score increase in the High participation group (largest among all groups)**

---

## 🧠 Key Findings

- Engagement does **not always lead to the highest final score**
- However, it strongly correlates with **score improvement**
- High participation groups show:
  - More consistent outcomes  
  - Stronger upward trends over time  

---

## ⚠️ Limitations

- Missing values in baseline and closing scores  
- Small sample sizes in some groups (e.g., Under 18)  
- Observational data — does not imply causation
